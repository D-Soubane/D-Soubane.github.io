Overview of Ph.D Thesis

My Ph.D project aims at unveiling the intriguing properties of silicon zero-dimensional nanostructures using DFT. The research focuses on light-matter interaction in nanostructured silicon to explore potential optical applications for these materials in advanced technologies.
