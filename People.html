Overview of Ph.D Thesis

My Ph.D project aims at unveiling the intriguing properties of silicon zero-dimensional nanostructures using DFT. We probe the light-matter interaction in nanostructured silicon to provide insight into the potential optical applications of these materials in cutting-edge technologies.
